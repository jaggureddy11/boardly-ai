The problem

Every departure starts with chaos

Millions of bus trips across India every day. And before every single one, the same broken ritual plays out.

01
The operator has to call everyone
One person. 30–50 passengers. Called 2–3 times each. That's over 100 manual calls before a single bus departs — while also managing the route, timing, and vehicle.
02
Passengers get panic-called
"Come fast, come fast." Every few minutes. No information about where the bus is. Just repeated pressure. Frustrating, dehumanizing — and sometimes leads to real arguments.
03
Apps haven't solved it
RedBus sends an SMS. AbhiBus shows a tracking link. But notifications don't interact. Operators still call manually. The gap is 100% unsolved by every major platform.
"The operator was calling every minute. My father got so irritated, they ended up arguing at the boarding point."

This isn't an edge case. It's the standard experience for hundreds of millions of bus passengers across India every year.

The problem isn't laziness or bad intentions. It's a communication gap that no technology has bothered to fill — until now.

This is exactly what Boardly fixes
How it works

Four steps. Zero manual calls.

Boardly runs automatically from the moment a journey is scheduled — no driver input needed.

1

Journey scheduled

Operator creates a trip or imports from their existing platform. Boardly automatically picks up the departure time and passenger list.

2

AI calls at the right time

30 minutes before departure, Boardly calls each passenger by name. Calm, professional, personalized — in their preferred language.

3

Listens and understands

"I'm on my way" → confirmed. "I'll be 10 minutes late" → flagged. "Where's the bus?" → live GPS response. Real conversation, not a robocall.

4

Operator sees everything

Real-time dashboard shows exactly who's confirmed, delayed, or unreachable — before the operator needs to pick up the phone.

Features

Not a notification system.
A conversation engine.

Every feature is designed around one goal: zero manual calls before boarding.

AI outbound calling

Automatically dials every passenger at 30 and 15 minutes before departure. No scheduling, no triggers — fully autonomous.

Core feature

Intent detection

AI understands what passengers say — "I'm coming", "I'll be late", "cancel my seat" — and takes action accordingly, automatically.

Powered by Groq AI

Live GPS in voice

"Where is my bus?" → AI fetches real-time location and responds naturally: "Your bus just crossed Silk Board and will arrive in 6 minutes."

Real-time data

Operator dashboard

Live view of every passenger's status: confirmed, delayed, unreachable. Updated in real-time as calls happen. No refreshing needed.

Real-time sync

SMS fallback

Call failed? Voicemail? Boardly automatically sends a concise SMS with all boarding details. 100% delivery guarantee.

Zero missed passengers

10+ Indian languages

Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi and more. Passenger's language is auto-detected from their booking — no setup required.
